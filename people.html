Meet the team of researchers, graduate students, and undergraduate students who make this work happen.

Dr. Stephanie Fryberg

Principal Investigator

Dr. Fryberg is a Professor of Psychology at the University of Michigan. Her primary research interests focus on how social representations of race, culture, and social class influence the development of self, psychological well-being, physical health, and educational attainment.
