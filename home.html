The Culture Collaboratory is dedicated to the study of culture and society. Our research focuses on understanding how culture influences individuals in a wide variety of domains, including identity development, educational attainment, health behaviors, and adversity responses.

We incorporate a range of methodologies in our research, including both field and laboratory studies, and explore questions using an interdisciplinary approach.
